Hello, I am Somchai. I have been walking these sugarcane rows all morning under a very hot sun, and I have some things to say about using this app on my iPhone 13.

MOBILE PAIN POINTS: Standing in the middle of the field at 10 AM, the glare on the screen is my biggest enemy. The white background is blinding, and the Thai text in those autocomplete dropdowns is too small for me to read without squinting. My hands are usually sweaty or dusty, so trying to tap a specific name in a tiny list with one thumb is frustrating; I often tap the person above or below the one I want. Also, because I open this through LINE, the browser bars at the top and bottom make the work area very cramped. I have to scroll too much just to find the "Submit" button, which is hard to do when I'm trying to hold my machete in the other hand.

SPEED ISSUES: Autocomplete sounds good on paper, but typing Thai characters on a small keyboard while walking between rows is slow. It takes me way more than 30 seconds if I have to type even a few letters for name, task, and location for every single worker. The bulk log is better, but having to re-select the "Task" and "Location" every time I make a new entry is a waste of time. Most of the time, I'm at the same plot all morning doing the same job—I shouldn't have to tell the app that 20 times.

ERRORS YOU WOULD MAKE: The full/half day toggle is very dangerous for me. It is easy to flip it by mistake while scrolling with my thumb, and I might not notice until the worker complains about their pay at the end of the month. If I submit a bulk log and realize I selected the wrong plot for all 25 people, I feel like throwing my phone into the cane because there is no easy "Undo" or "Edit All" button. I have to go into the logs and fix them one by one, which I simply don't have time for.

MISSING FEATURES: I really need a "Same as Yesterday" button. My crew doesn't change much day-to-day. If I could just load yesterday's list and untap the two people who didn't show up, I would be done in 10 seconds. I also wish the app would use my GPS to just know which plot I am standing in. If I am at Plot A, just set the location to Plot A automatically. Also, a high-contrast mode or "Field Mode" with giant buttons and black background would help me see through the sunlight.

TOP 3 FEATURE REQUESTS:
1. Copy Yesterday's Attendance: This is the #1 time saver. Loading the previous day's successful log and just making quick edits would save me the most effort every single morning.
2. GPS Auto-Location for Plots: If the app automatically selects the plot based on where I am standing, that's one less dropdown I have to fight with one hand.
3. Big "Select All" and "Undo" for Bulk Logs: Tapping 25 cards individually is still too many taps. I need a "Select All" button and a way to instantly cancel a mistake I just submitted.
