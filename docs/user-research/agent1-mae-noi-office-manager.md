Sawatdee-ka, I am Mae Noi. I've been using this system on my Samsung tablet every morning at 7am when the workers gather at the truck. While it's better than my old paper notebook, I have some feedback from the field here in Khon Kaen.

FRICTION POINTS: The biggest headache is the repetitive data entry in the morning rush. Even with autocomplete, having to select the task and location for every single entry or bulk group is slow when I have 20 people staring at me. In sugarcane work, the "Location" (like Plot A or the East field) and the "Task" (like weeding or cutting) usually stay the same for 3 or 4 days in a row. Re-selecting "Cutting" and "East Plot" every single morning feels like a waste of time. Also, the Full/Half day toggle is a bit small for my fingers when I'm walking around the dusty field; I often miss-tap it.

MISSING FEATURES: I really need a "Rainy Day" button. If it starts pouring at 10am, everyone stops. I need a way to quickly mark that the whole crew only worked a quarter-day or stopped due to weather without editing every person one by one. I also desperately need a "Cash Advance" or "Bia-liang" tracker. My workers always ask for 100 or 200 baht for gas or rice during the week, and currently, I still have to write these in a separate paper notebook because the app only calculates the final total at the end of the month.

UX ISSUES: That 7-second undo toast message actually gets in the way. It pops up right over the navigation buttons at the bottom of my Chrome browser, so I have to wait for it to disappear before I can move to the next screen. For someone like me who wants to finish and go get coffee, those 7 seconds feel like an eternity. Also, the autocomplete can be dangerous if I have two workers named "Somchai"—if the app doesn't show their nicknames or last initials clearly in the dropdown, I might log the wrong one.

NICE TO HAVE: I would love a "Copy from Yesterday" button. Most of the time, the same 15 regulars are doing exactly what they did yesterday. If I could just tap "Same as Yesterday" and then just uncheck the two people who didn't show up, I would be done in 30 seconds instead of 10 minutes.

TOP 3 FEATURE REQUESTS:
1. Duplicate Yesterday's Logs: This would save me the most time by letting me just adjust the few things that changed rather than starting from scratch every morning.
2. Cash Advance/Deduction Column: I need to record the money I give them mid-month so the "Print Payroll" button actually shows the correct amount left to pay.
3. Photo Upload for Outsource Logs: I want to take a photo of the contractor's work or their handwritten receipt so the farm owner can see proof of the expense immediately.
