Top 10 feature recommendations ranked by cross-persona demand and real-world impact.

1. Cash Advance and Deduction Tracking (Mae Noi, Wanchai, Nong Fah): Adds a ledger to record mid-month payments and loans that automatically deduct from final payroll totals. Build complexity: M.

2. Duplicate Yesterday's Logs (Mae Noi, Somchai): A one-tap button that pre-fills the current day's attendance based on the previous day's successful entries to minimize repetitive data entry. Build complexity: S.

3. Excel/CSV Data Export (Nong Fah): Allows accountants to download all log and payroll data in spreadsheet formats for formal reporting and tax preparation. Build complexity: S.

4. Unit-based and Piece-rate Logging (Wanchai): Enables logging work by output (e.g., bundles cut, tons loaded, or rai weeded) rather than just daily or half-day increments. Build complexity: M.

5. Harvest Tonnage and CCS Tracker (Lung Prasert): Records the weight and sugar content (CCS) of sugarcane delivered to the mill to track revenue against plot costs. Build complexity: M.

6. Fertilizer and Chemical Application Log (Lung Prasert, Nong Fah): Tracks the date, plot, formula, and quantity of fertilizer applied to ensure crop nutrition schedules are met and costs are captured. Build complexity: M.

7. Seasonal Reporting and Custom Date Ranges (Lung Prasert, Nong Fah): Allows users to generate cost and labor reports across a full 10-12 month crop cycle rather than being limited to calendar months. Build complexity: M.

8. Worker Document and Permit Expiry Alerts (Wanchai): Stores passport and work permit details for labor compliance and triggers notifications before legal documents expire. Build complexity: M.

9. Crew Leader and Worker Grouping (Wanchai, Mae Noi): Organizes the worker list into sub-teams or crews to allow for faster bulk-selection based on existing field hierarchies. Build complexity: S.

10. High-Contrast Field Mode (Somchai, Mae Noi): Introduces a dark-themed UI with enlarged buttons and high-visibility text to combat screen glare during midday field use. Build complexity: S.
